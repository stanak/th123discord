Main Commands Supported by Shanghai-bot¶

Try the !help command first. Shanghai will send you a DM which includes a brief list of the latest commands available. !help can also takes other commands as arguments to provide detailed information of them, for example, !help host, !help ch join, and more.

th123-related Commands¶

!host <ip_address>:<port_no> <message>

Registers your host/client information for th123 matches to the #hostlist channel. Shanghai-bot will reply to you when she accepts your registration.

You can use this command only via DMs to Shanghai-bot.

Example. “!host 198.51.100.123:10800 Let’s enjoy games!”

Note

After registration, Shanghai-bot automatically checks if your host is accessible or not.
If Shanghai-bot cannot find any accessible host with the given information, she will reply to you and automatically cancel your registration.
!client <message>

Registers your host/client information for th123 matches to the #hostlist channel. Shanghai-bot will reply to you when she accepts your registration.

You can use this command only via DMs to Shanghai-bot.

Example. “!client Please tell me your IP via DM!”

Note

The registered message will remain for 1 hour, during which you can wait for kinds of events where someone gives you an IP for games, where someone proposes you to try matches with Hamachi, and so on.

!close

Removes explicitly your registration in the #hostlist channel created by the host comman or the client one.

You can use this command only via DMs to Shanghai-bot.

Example. “!close”

Discord Channel Utility Commands¶

!ch

Gives you many functions related to hidden channels. Combine one of the following subcommands depending on what you want.

To join a hidden channel called “dev”: “!ch join dev”

!ch join <channel_name>

Lets you join the hidden channel named <channel_name>. You cannot use this command via DMs to Shanghai-bot.

To join a hidden channel called “dev”: “!ch join dev”

!ch leave <channel_name>

Lets you leave the hidden channel named <channel_name>. You cannot use this command via DMs to Shanghai-bot.

To leave the hidden channel named “dev”: “!ch leave dev”

!ch list

Shows a brief list of hidden channels with each number of attendees.

Example. “!ch list”
