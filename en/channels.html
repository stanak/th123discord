This category consists of channels for management of this server itself: brief information of the server, requirements to managers, questionnaires, and so.

#information

Guides to this HTML with a brief introduction.

#announcements

Lists recent announcements from the server manager, including the recent update history.

Provides a place for arguments, opinions, requirements about the server. You can send a request for a new channel here as well.

#dev

A hidden channel where developers and contributors conduct technical talks, for example, about improvements of Shanghai-bot.

TH123 CategoryÂ¶

Channels for more general topics related to th123.

#soku-general

A channel for general topics related to th123. Even topics not directly related to th123 will be allowed as long as they are related to th123 to some extent.

A channel placed for th123 beginners (up to 2 years from starting, as a rough indication).

A channel where players talk after matches for improvement of their moves. Here some people upload their replays and wait for pieces of advice of skilled players and others plan to schedule a tournament and consult skilled planners.

You can advertise live-streaming URLs, mainly related to th123.

#wiki

A channel where editors of the th123 Wiki to share their editing activities and tips.
